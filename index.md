## About Me


![Profile Picture]({{ "assets/images/profile-pic.jpg" | relative_url }}){: class="profile-image"}

I’m expected to graduate with my Bachelor of Science (BS) degree and academic minor in May 2027 in addition to achieving a Master of Engineering (MEng) degree in Aerospace Engineering by December 2027.

I have an extensive background as a lead in an engineering project team working with Hyperloop technology, undergraduate research developing a thermoelectric solar furnace prototype, an engineering asset management internship at NYC DEP, and a passion for peer mentorship. I love working on data, consulting, automotives, aerospace, and the intersection between tech and successful business models to improve the future and happiness of our world.
